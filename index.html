The best Pavlova



Ingridients

  • 3egg whites
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 cup white suggar
  • 1 tbs cronstarch
  • 1 teaspoon of lemon juice

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C). Line a sheet pan with parchment paper. Draw a 9 inch circle on the parchment. An easy way to do this is to draw around the outside of a 9 inch pan with a pencil.
  2. In a large bowl, beat egg whites on high speed until soft peaks form. Add 3/4 cup of the sugar gradually, while continuing to whip. Make sure sugar is completely dissolved. Mix together the remaining 1/4 cup sugar with the cornstarch; lightly fold into meringue with lemon juice."
  3. "Spread a layer of meringue to fit a circle on parchment,approximately 1/4 inch thick. With the remainder of mixture, a pipe or spoon swirls around the edges to form a shallow bowl shape.
  4. In a large bowl, combine the cream and half a cup of confectioners sugar, and whip until thickened. Decorate with fruit of your choice; strawberries are excellent.
